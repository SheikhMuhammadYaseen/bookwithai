Welcome to the Interactive Learning Platform for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics.

This digital book provides a comprehensive introduction to the exciting world of embodied artificial intelligence. You will learn about the fundamental concepts, core technologies, and practical applications of building intelligent robots that can operate in the real world.

Navigate through the chapters using the sidebar to explore topics ranging from ROS2 and sensor integration to advanced simulation with NVIDIA Isaac and the principles of humanoid robot design.

Let's begin this journey into the future of robotics!

# Chapter 11: AI Perception Integration in Humanoids

## 11.1 The Role of AI in Humanoid Perception {#ch11-sec1-role-of-ai}

AI plays a crucial role in humanoid perception. It allows robots to make sense of the vast amounts of data that they collect from their sensors. This is a very challenging task, as the real world is a very complex and dynamic environment.

## 11.2 Computer Vision for Humanoid Robots {#ch11-sec2-computer-vision}

Computer vision is a field of AI that deals with how computers can be made to gain high-level understanding from digital images or videos. This is a very important area of research for humanoid robotics, as it allows robots to "see" and "understand" the world around them.

## 11.3 Natural Language Processing for Humanoid Robots {#ch11-sec3-nlp}

Natural language processing (NLP) is a field of AI that deals with how computers can be made to understand and generate human language. This is another important area of research for humanoid robotics, as it allows robots to communicate with humans in a more natural way.

## 11.4 The Future of AI in Humanoid Robotics {#ch11-sec4-future-of-ai}

The integration of AI is transforming the field of humanoid robotics. This section will discuss some of the latest trends in this area and explore some of the potential applications for AI-powered humanoid robots in the future.
